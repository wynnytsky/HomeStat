If you see someone in need of

homeless assistance,

call 311 or use the 311 Mobile App.

Give the exact location of the individual and time you observed them. Describe what they look like, what they are doing and the condition that is prompting your request.

The Department of Homeless Services Outreach Providers will attempt to locate the person in need of assistance and offer them services including transportation to shelter, a bed in transitional housing, assistance in gathering vital documents, applying for public benefits, and receiving permanent housing.

In case of emergency, dial 911.
